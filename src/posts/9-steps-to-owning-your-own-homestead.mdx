Dreaming of owning a homestead but not sure how to get there? In this video, we share the practical steps that moved us from city life toward our family homestead: articulating the dream, researching cities, setting a realistic income target, buying what we could afford, budgeting hard, taking wise counsel, making the right real estate leap, being willing to suffer through the messy middle, building a trusted team, and taking the homestead one step at a time.

## What we learned

This project is the kind of thing we started documenting because homesteading advice can get weirdly polished online. Real life has kids leaving gates open, water freezing at the worst possible moment, animals discovering the one weak spot in the fence, and adults realizing they should have measured twice before buying once.

The big takeaway from this video: build the system so normal people can keep using it when everyone is tired. If a chore only works when the most motivated adult remembers every step, it is not a system yet. It is a wish wearing boots.

## Field notes summary

- The homestead dream started with naming the goal clearly before chasing property listings.
- Planning included location research, budgeting, debt decisions, proximity to family, and what kind of life the family actually wanted.
- The video is less about a magic formula and more about patient, practical steps toward land and a simpler life.
- The takeaway: a homestead begins as a family vision, then becomes spreadsheets, tradeoffs, savings, and steady obedience to the next right step.
